# 3/21/2026 12 PM - More research and major redesign

_Time spent: 6h_

ok so a lot happened today. I was going back and forth trying to figure out the best components and i landed on something really really good.
so basically the old design had IR2104 gate drivers which wont fit on a 26x26mm board so i had to rethink everything. did a ton of research and found way better components.
new component list:

STM32G473CEU6 (same chip as the real Matrix FC)
ICM-42688-P (better gyro than MPU6000, more resistant to RF noise)
4x DRV8323RS (replaces all 12 IR2104s + reduces component count massively)
8x FS8205A dual MOSFETs
AT7456E OSD
ESP32 C3 WROOM 02 for AI link
MP1584EN buck regulator
2x XC6220B331 clean LDOs

the biggest change is the AI link. originally i was gonna put the raspberry pi on the drone but that would make it too heavy for the 0802SE motors. so the pi stays on the ground and the ESP32 on the drone handles the wireless link using ESP-NOW protocol which has under 2ms latency. way faster than wifi.
also half these components arent in kicad so had to make custom symbols again which took ages.
still gotta finish the schematic but its looking way more professional than before ngl
